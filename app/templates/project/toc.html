Theory of Change

If talented Tanzanian youth receive equitable access to higher education through scholarships, and are supported academically and through engagement opportunities, then they will graduate with strong academic and professional skills, which leads to improved employability, enhanced civic participation, and long-term national development.

Problem Statement

Talented Tanzanian youth lack access to higher education and mentorship, hindering their academic and professional growth. This limits their employability, civic participation, and contribution to national development.
